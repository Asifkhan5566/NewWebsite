Nigran-e-Suraha Of Dawat-e-Islami
Maulana Haji Imran Attari

Maulana Imran Attari is a world-renowned Islamic Scholar. He has been the Head of the Central Advisory Board of the global movement for the preaching of Quran and Sunnah, Dawat-e-Islami for around 18 years. Through his brilliant vision and leadership under the guidance of the great Sufi saint of this century. Maulana Imran Attari was born on Wednesday, 8th July, 1970 in Karachi. After finishing his school and higher business studies, he started taking part in his father's business and later on, founded his own garment business. He joined Dawat-e-Islami in 1992 as a local volunteer and was appointed the Head of the Central Advisory Board of Dawat-e-Islami by His Eminence Maulana Ilyas Qadiri in 2002. He has been invited to deliver lectures in various parts of the U.K, Sri Lanka, Singapore, Indonesia, Germany, South Africa, Kenya, Uganda, Belgium, Austria, Netherlands, Norway, Portugal, Italy, Greece, Mozambique and many other parts of the world.
